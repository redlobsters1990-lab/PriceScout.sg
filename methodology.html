Methodology

How PriceScout.sg evaluates comparison categories

PriceScout.sg focuses on practical product categories where comparison content helps users make a clearer buying decision. Our content structure is designed to be concise, transparent, and useful at a glance.

Core evaluation principles

  • Buyer-intent categories are prioritized over informational topics.
  • Structured data matters: price, specs, features, merchant availability, and fit-for-use are central.
  • Singapore relevance matters, including local merchant context and practical purchasing fit.
  • Clarity matters more than volume. We aim for readable comparison layouts instead of bloated content.

What a strong comparison page includes

  • Clear introduction and product context
  • Quick comparison table
  • Top options and differences
  • Price comparison overview
  • Pros and cons
  • Where to buy / affiliate links where relevant
  • Buying guide and FAQ support

Affiliate disclosure

Some pages may contain affiliate links. If you click through and purchase, PriceScout.sg may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This helps support the maintenance of the site.
